Steak

Steak is a cut of beef that is typically sliced from the muscle of the cow, often grilled, pan-seared, or broiled. It is known for its tender texture and rich flavor, and can vary in thickness, cooking method, and degree of doneness, such as rare, medium, or well-done.

Pork Chop

A pork chop is a cut of meat from the loin of a pig, typically including a bone and varying in thickness. It can be grilled, pan-fried, or baked, and is known for its tender texture and savory flavor. Pork chops are often seasoned with spices or marinades and are commonly served with sides like vegetables or potatoes.

Tiramisu Cake

Tiramisu cake is a dessert inspired by the classic Italian tiramisu. It typically consists of layers of light, spongy cake (often made with ladyfingers or sponge cake) soaked in coffee or espresso, and layered with a rich mascarpone cream filling. The cake is often flavored with cocoa powder and sometimes a splash of liqueur like rum or coffee liqueur. It has a delicate balance of sweet, creamy, and coffee flavors.
